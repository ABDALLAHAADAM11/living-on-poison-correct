Junk food refers to highly processed and unhealthy food that is typically high in calories, sugar, unhealthy fats, and low in essential nutrients. While it may be tempting and convenient to indulge in these types of foods, consuming them regularly can have detrimental effects on our health. The bad effects of junk food can manifest in various ways, impacting both our physical and mental well-being.
